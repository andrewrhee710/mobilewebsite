Hello, we are LE SSERAFIM!

Le Sserafim is a five member (formerly six) group under HYBE Entertainment, with Source Music being the sub-label handing the group's production, press, and group activities. Known for their unapologetic boldness, Le Sserafim can be instantly characterized in one's mind by their song titles. "Fearless", "Antifragile", "The Hydra", and more, it's clear that this group is not afraid to express their true selves to the world. They debuted with "Fearless" in May 2022, garnering huge hype and attention as (similar to IVE), they have two former IZ*ONE member in their group: Sakura and Chaewon.

mang2goon, (CC BY 3.0), 2022

Very similar to the other two gorups, Le Sserafim gained popularity thanks to their unique persona and style of music. Listeners around the world were tired of the widespread use of loud, powerful EDM style music being produced by most K-pop groups. Le Sserafim not only brought a more chill, down-low style to their songs, the lyrics and the message sent by the song was a bold and powerful one for the K-pop community. "What you lookin' at?" and "I'm fearless" conquered users headphones, and while some criticized the song for being "boring" and "underwhelming", the song and choreography say otherwise.
